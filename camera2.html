Details

This instant camera was built using refurbished internal components — sourced directly from vintage Polaroid instant cameras made in the 1980s and ‘90s. The Polaroid Sun660 Autofocus Instant Camera is a game-changing piece of photographic equipment, featuring advanced sonar technology that delivers exceptional results. This camera was Polaroid's first to combine 600 type film with the innovative Sonar Autofocus system, which uses ultrasonic sound waves to adjust focus automatically.

The high-speed capabilities of 600 film, combined with a built-in flash, make this camera a versatile tool that can excel in nearly any shooting situation. Whether you're capturing a candid moment, taking a portrait, or shooting in low light, the Polaroid 600 Sun660 delivers exceptional results. With a durable and compact design, it is perfect for on-the-go photography, ensuring you never miss a moment. To use this camera, all you need is a pack of Polaroid 600 film (sold separately) and you’ll be able to experience instant photography at its most authentic — on an original Polaroid vintage camera.
