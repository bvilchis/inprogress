Details

This vintage Polaroid 600 instant camera from the 1990s has been refurbished and rebuilt by Retrospekt, and is ready for another generation of photo taking. All you need is a pack of Polaroid 600 film (sold separately) and you’ll be able to experience instant photography at its most authentic — on an original Polaroid vintage camera!

The high speed of 600 film, which requires less light, combined with a built-in flash and fixed focusing, form an incredibly versatile instant film camera that’s easy to use and ready to excel in nearly any situation. Don't forget: Polaroid film is sensitive to light, so keep it shielded for 3 minutes (upside-down, in a pocket, etc.) while it's developing so you can get the most vivid and colorful result.
