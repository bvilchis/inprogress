Details

The Impulse was Polaroid's first major reimagining of what a 600 instant film camera could be. It features a compact design, making it more convenient to store and, thanks to its binocular-style grips, easier to hold and shoot. It also has a pop-up flash that protects the lens when not in use. This vintage Polaroid Impulse camera from the 1980s has been refurbished and rebuilt by our team, and is ready for another generation of photo taking. All you need is a pack of Polaroid 600 film (sold separately) and you’ll be able to experience instant photography at its most authentic — on an original Polaroid vintage camera!

The high speed of 600 film, which requires less light, combined with a built-in flash and fixed focusing, form an incredibly versatile instant film camera that’s easy to use and ready to excel in nearly any situation. Don't forget: Polaroid film is sensitive to light, so keep it shielded for 3 minutes (upside-down, in a pocket, etc.) while it's developing so you can get the most vivid and colorful result.
