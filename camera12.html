Bright and bold like a leap into Donut Pond — this vibrant Polaroid 600 instant film camera captures the playful spirit of Keroppi (けろけろけろっぴ) at his most adventurous. Created by Retrospekt and Sanrio as part of their beloved retro tech collection, every piece in this series features lively green tones and official Keroppi artwork, celebrating 35 years of this much-beloved character with delightful details inspired by his colorful world.

This camera combines refurbished vintage Polaroid internal components from the 80s and 90s with a freshly-molded exterior designed and built by Retrospekt. Enjoy the classic instant photography experience with modern reliability, all while contributing to sustainability by giving new life to vintage parts.
